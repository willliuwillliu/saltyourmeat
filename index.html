          About

          This meat salting/dry brining calculator is heavily inspired by Samin's salting guidelines in Salt Fat Acid Heat. She opened my eyes to the importance of dry brining (a fancy way of saying salting in advance), and I've never looked back.

          I made this calculator because I always salt meat in advance now, but I'm usually too lazy to pull out her chart and end up getting nervous about over or under salting. So here we are!
